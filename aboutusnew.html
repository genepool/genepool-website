About GenePool

GenePool is a website that was conceived by a team of four high school students at the end of a seven-week intensive
coding program powered by Girls Who Code. Girls Who Code is an organization trying to close the gender gap in the tech
industry.

Our website was made with the goals of identifying illnesses for which individuals would be at risk based on their genetic
makeup as well as their location. With GenePool, we hope to identify and prevent any possible illnesses in its first stages,
or even before its appearance.

In the future, we hope GenePool will be able to take in family medical history as another factor, and create an easy,
personalized experience. Through this, you’ll be able to identify genetic diseases for which you are at risk, as well as
any other members of your family.
